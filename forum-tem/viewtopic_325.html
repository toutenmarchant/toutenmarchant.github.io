Micro cravate ME2 (Sennheiser) + bonette

de Thierry le Dim 9 Mai 2010 13:36


Prix d'achat : 90,04

Utilisation combinée avec L'enregistreur Bullet DVR

Description donnée par le magasin :

The Sennheiser ME 2 is an omnidirectional electret-condenser lavalier mic with cable, clip and grille. Ideal for theatre and presentation use, the ME 2 clip-on microphone is virtually invisible.
It is supplied with a 1/8 inch (3.5mm) Mini (TRS) Output Jack for use with Evolution G2 series wireless systems. It can be powered directly from the SK 100 G2, SK 300 G2 or SK 500 G2 bodypack transmitters. It can also powered directly using Phantom power with the optional MZA 900 P.

Sennheiser ME 2 Features:
  • 1/8 inch (3.5mm) Mini (TRS) Output Jack
  • Works with Sennheiser Evolution 100, 300 and 500 series bodypack transmitters
  • Works with Phantom Power using the optional MZA 900 P
  • Renowned Sennheiser warm, clear and robust broadcast quality sound reproduction

Sennheiser ME 2 Specifications:
  • Transducer: Principle condenser
  • Sensitivity: 20 mV/Pa
  • Pick-up pattern: Omni-driectional
  • Max. SPL: 130 dB
